About - Care to Shine

Care to Shine was created because our key team members, who have worked in the disability and education industry for many years, wanted to provide better service to the people they support. We feel that our participants deserve exceptional care that is person centred and focused on meeting individual needs. 

We value and promote a collaborative and partnership approach with parents, guardians and all stakeholders and we want our participants' families to feel safe and supported by the services they receive.

Care To Shine is a small family orientated service provider who views support as holistic, which includes all family members. Our mission is to help parents and guardians know their loved ones have a future beyond being just cared for!

Our Services

At Care to Shine we provide a broad range of services.
The core services that we provide are, In Home Support, Community Participation, Supported Independent Living and Short term Accommodation.

Free NDIS Consultation

Care to Shine offers any person with a disability, a free one hour consultation with an industry expert for advice relating to:
- Accessing the NDIS
- Navigating the scheme
- Implementing your NDIS plan
- How to best utilise your funding
- Preparing for planning meetings

Community Participation

We believe that participants social lives are important to their wellbeing and quality of life. Community access and engagement enables opportunities for connection and growth. We support our participants to access their local community and engage in opportunities to achieve gainful employment, social and recreational activities. We deliver our services and types of activities based on the needs and interests of those whom we support.

Supported Independent Living (SIL)

We provide independent living support in many different ways throughout South East Queensland. Whether it's your own home, or one of the many private homes we have access to, our goal is for you to be comfortable in a home that supports your desired lifestyle. Our independent or shared house model is designed to nurture, engage and teach our participants skills to help them live the life they choose. 

Independant Living Options (ILO)

Care To Shine can provide an individualised tailored package of support, living in the environment of your choice. ILO services are an alternative to Supported Independent Living models, which combine both primary and supplementary support without the rigid structure of traditional housing support models. Ask us how we can help you design your forever home.

Short term Accommodation

Need a mini break?
We provide Short term Accommodation that is fully self contained, offers full meal service and is supported by our 24 hour highly trained staff. You can have piece of mind and a well earned break knowing your loved ones are cared for and indulging in fun activities personally tailored to them.

In Home Support

We provide excellent individualised support and care. We go above and beyond normal service provision expectations and build a collaborative and partnership approach to supporting our participants. We help develop an individual care plan to match your requirements and staffing needs. And most importantly, we are open to feedback to achieve a positive result.
